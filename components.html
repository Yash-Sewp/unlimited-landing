Common mechanical or electronic components
that are covered include:

Gearbox

Grinding gears, difficulty shifting, or unexpected slippage can all be signs of a failing gearbox. These issues can not only disrupt your driving experience, but also lead to expensive repairs.

Cooling System

Is your car overheating, experiencing low coolant levels, or displaying a temperature gauge warning? These could indicate a cooling system failure. A malfunctioning cooling system can lead to significant engine damage.

Sensors

Is your check engine light on? Does your car seem sluggish or hesitate during acceleration? These could be signs of a malfunctioning sensor. Sensors play a vital role in optimizing engine performance and fuel efficiency.

Fuel System

Is your car experiencing hesitation, stalling, or failing to start entirely? These symptoms could point to a fuel system malfunction. Don’t get caught off guard by a fuel pump failure or clogged injectors.

Audio Systems

Does your favorite music sound distorted or lifeless? Are you experiencing crackling noises or complete silence? These could indicate an audio system failure.

Engine

Engine failure can leave you stranded and facing significant repair costs. Our comprehensive warranties provide coverage for this unforeseen event, including internal component malfunctions and related labor charges.
